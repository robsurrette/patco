Patco Train Schedule

Clean, intuitive, and enormously helpful

A must have for Patco users, this app includes all the necessary information that riders need every day. Check it out on the app store!

Features & Details

Built for commuters. Designed for ease of use.

Find Upcoming Trains

View the schedule of upcoming train times in a clean and easy to read format.

Countdown to Next Train

The amount of time until each train departs is listed underneath the time.

View the Station Map

View a map of all Patco stations to help you plan your trip.

Works Offline

No internet connection needed to search upcoming train times as well as all other features.

Find Fares and Prices

Find the one way or round trip ticket cost for your Patco trip.

Reload Your Freedom Card

Add funds to your Freedom Card so you can easily navigate the stations.
